About Us

Welcome to Oryx Group Media, the latest venture of the award-winning Oryx Group WLL, a Qatar-registered company renowned for its expertise in entertainment, events, media services, and hospitality. Oryx Group Media is dedicated to fostering intercultural, social, and economic engagement between Qatar and Africa through innovative online media programs.

Our platform offers a first-of-its-kind audiovisual omnichannel experience that bridges Arabian and African communities in Qatar and beyond. We will deliver a variety of informative and knowledge-based programs, streamed across major social media platforms, including YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, X (Twitter), TikTok, Spotify, and Apple Podcasts.
